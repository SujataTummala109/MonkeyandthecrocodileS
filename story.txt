**Story**
Once upon a time there lived a monkey who resided on a berry tree, which was on the banks of a river. In the river there lived a crocodile and his wife......
The monkey wanted to make a cloth of himself using crocodile's skin.
He slowly started trying to befriend the crocodile by feeding him berries. The berries were deilicious AND the crocodile liked it. After some time the monkey started poisoning the berries.....
